About Us

Christ School, Bengaluru is a Christian minority institution with all the rights and privileges granted by the constitution of India. The school is an unaided institution recognized by the department of education, Karnataka. It is managed by the fathers of Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI) who run hundreds of educational institutions of national and international repute including Christ University. The School is governed by a registered body ‘Christ Educational Trust’ Bengaluru.
